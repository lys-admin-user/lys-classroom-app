## Product Vision: Project Pulse (Working Title)

This system serves as a unified, data-driven operations engine designed for a scaling organization. By sitting directly between **HubSpot** and **Google Workspace**, it bridges external customer acquisition with internal team execution.

Unlike rigid project management tools, this platform treats internal team onboarding, role progression, and customer success journeys as interconnected workflows, ensuring no context is lost as the team grows.

---

### Core Architecture & The "Handshake" Engine

The system relies on real-time automated data pipelines to remove manual administrative overhead during critical growth inflection points:

* **The Customer Onboarding Pipeline:** The moment a deal is marked **"Closed Won" in HubSpot**, the system intercepts the webhook, parses the associated company data, and automatically provisions a role-specific customer onboarding board. This ensures the delivery team instantly inherits the exact context established during the sales cycle.
* **The Internal HR Pipeline:** The creation of a new user account in **Google Admin / the LYS App** serves as the single source of truth for headcount. It instantly triggers a standardized, role-specific employee onboarding timeline. Simultaneously, it maps the new hire to their respective team and individual dashboards, completely automating the Day 1 provisioning experience.

---

### Modular Visual Hierarchy

Instead of forcing a single data structure into restrictive layouts, the platform uses an independent, multi-module database layer. This allows teams to track progress through completely different operational lenses while keeping statuses synchronized.

```
                  ┌──────────────────────────────┐
                  │   Central Database Engine    │
                  └──────────────┬───────────────┘
         ┌───────────────────────┼───────────────────────┐
         ▼                       ▼                       ▼
┌─────────────────┐     ┌─────────────────┐     ┌─────────────────┐
│  Journey Map    │     │  Kanban Boards  │     │ Master Calendar │
│ (Milestones &   │     │  (Day-to-Day    │     │ (Macro Targets  │
│  Phases View)   │     │  Task Delivery) │     │  & Deadlines)   │
└─────────────────┘     └─────────────────┘     └─────────────────┘

```

* **The Customer/Employee Journey Map:** A high-level, linear phase view (e.g., *Phase 1: Discovery/Orientation → Phase 2: Technical Integration → Phase 3: Adoption*). It tracks macro milestones, client sentiment, and overall health scores rather than minor daily tasks.
* **The Kanban Boards:** The granular execution layer. This is the day-to-day workspace where tasks are moved through custom pipelines (e.g., *Backlog, In Progress, In Review, Done*). While independent, a "Blocker" task on a Kanban board can automatically flag a corresponding phase on the Journey Map as "At Risk."
* **The Master Calendar:** A time-bound visual grid highlighting critical delivery deadlines, launch dates, and company-wide compliance timelines, establishing a predictable operational rhythm.

---

### Tri-Level Visibility Framework

To protect team focus while maintaining organizational alignment, the interface uses a responsive toggle framework that instantly filters noise based on the user's immediate needs:

| View Level | Primary Target Audience | Core Functionality |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **1. The Zoom-Out View** | Founders & Leadership | A birds-eye, macro perspective of the annual calendar, high-level journey maps for all clients, and aggregated team health metrics. |
| **2. The Team View** | Department / Project Leads | Filters data down to a specific cross-functional group. Focuses on the group's active Kanban boards, shared milestones, and workload balancing. |
| **3. The Individual View** | Individual Contributors | A hyper-focused, distraction-free cockpit showing only the specific user's daily tasks, personal onboarding milestones, and immediate deadlines. |
