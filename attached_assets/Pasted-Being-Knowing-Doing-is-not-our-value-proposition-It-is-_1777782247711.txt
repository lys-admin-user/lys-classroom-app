Needs Analyzer Recommendation
The Strategic Purpose: Route visitors from "What is this?" to "This is exactly for me" in under 60 seconds — before price ever enters the conversation.
The 4-Question Flow:
Q1 — Identity (Who are you?) Student / Parent or Guardian / Teacher or Educator / School or Administrator / Homeschool Family / Community or Youth Organization
Q2 — Core Pain (What's your biggest challenge right now?) Options dynamically shift based on Q1 — this is critical. A parent sees "I don't know how to guide my child's next step." A teacher sees "Lesson planning is consuming my time."
Q3 — Urgency (Where are you in the journey?) Just starting to explore / Actively looking for a solution / Ready to get started now
Q4 — Desired Outcome (What does success look like for you?) Again, dynamically driven by Q1 — outcome language should mirror their segment's gains from the canvas.
The Result Page should do three things:
Reflect their answers back in their own language — "Based on what you shared, here's what LYS can do for you..."
Lead with the segment-specific value proposition, not a product list
Give one clear next step — not three options, not a menu. One: Book a demo / Start free / Download the guide / Join the Parents Circle
