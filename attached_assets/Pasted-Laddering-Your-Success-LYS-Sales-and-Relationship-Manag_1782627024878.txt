Laddering Your Success (LYS) Sales and Relationship Manager, Study Abroad SOP & KPI Framework (Pre-Launch 2025–2026)
1. Executive Summary
The LYS Global Exchange & Study Abroad Program Team is responsible for establishing the foundational systems, partnerships, and operational readiness required to launch the Laddering Your Success (LYS) Study Abroad initiative. This initiative will connect American institutions—particularly Historically Black Colleges and Universities (HBCUs)—with partners in West and North Africa to foster cultural exchange, mutual learning, and the teaching of the LYS Be–Know–Do (BKD) methodology. 
The program will serve as a bridge between American educational institutions and African organizations, supporting academic collaboration, cultural immersion, and professional development opportunities. During the pre-launch phase (2025–2026), the LYS team will focus on building scalable systems, securing partnerships, and developing the program curriculum and infrastructure necessary for launch readiness by 2026.
Operational Priority: Establish a solid foundation for global educational exchange through strategic planning, partnership development, compliance readiness, and alignment with the LYS mission and core values of Commitment, Wisdom, and Compassion.  LYS will serve as a mediator for educational institutions and programs in both the  US and Africa, and will offer its transformational BKD methodology to enrollees.

3. KPIs (Key Performance Indicators)
Operational KPIs
These KPIs track the daily and weekly progress of the team responsible for developing the program infrastructure, vendor systems, and communication channels necessary for program readiness.
• Daily response time to partner and vendor inquiries: within 24 hours
• Minimum 2 new partner or vendor leads logged per day
• 100% daily updates in project management/CRM system
• Weekly readiness meetings held with summary reports
Partnership & Outreach KPIs
• 3–5 new HBCU or institutional partnerships per quarter
• 5–10 MoUs signed with African universities, high schools, or NGOs per quarter
• Minimum 2 partnership meetings or webinars per week
• Partnership retention rate ≥ 85% annually
Program Infrastructure KPIs
• Global Operations Manual completed within 90 days pre-launch
• Vendor database of minimum 10 approved partners established
• All travel, legal, and insurance documents 100% compliant before launch
• CRM and communication systems fully operational by mid-2025
Financial & Compliance KPIs
• 100% budget reconciliation within ±5% variance per quarter
• All contracts, waivers, and insurance forms verified and archived pre-launch
• Quarterly financial reports submitted on time
Marketing & Visibility KPIs
• 1 public-facing feature (webinar/blog/podcast) per semester
• Social engagement growth +10% per month
• Marketing materials and outreach decks completed by Q2 2025
4. SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures)
Daily Tasks
• Respond to all student, partner, and vendor messages within 24 hours.
• Log all communications, leads, and expenses in CRM or project tracker.
• Monitor vendor and travel updates; confirm safety and visa status changes.
• Share daily updates in internal team communication channel.
Weekly Tasks
• Conduct weekly readiness and strategy meetings with leadership.
• Review partnership and vendor progress; update milestone tracker.
• Submit Weekly Readiness Report summarizing progress and challenges.
• Verify budget updates and document compliance logs.
Monthly Tasks
• Finalize and sign at least one partnership MoU per month.
• Conduct vendor and partnership performance reviews.
• Publish one monthly program development update (internal or public).
• Submit Monthly Operations Report to leadership.
Semester-Based Tasks
• Host at least one virtual or in-person partnership roundtable with institutions.
• Evaluate program readiness milestones and update SOP manual.
• Prepare pre-launch orientation or pilot planning documents.
Yearly Tasks
• Conduct annual strategic review and publish the Global Exchange Impact Report.
• Review all partnerships and vendor agreements for renewal or expansion.
• Host Annual LYS Global Exchange Strategy Retreat.
