 Lesson Close 

 Dichotomous keys are tools that are used to help classify organisms. They consist of at least two opposing statements that describe characteristics that can be used to sort organisms into groups. 

 Connection to Education Domain 
 When is curiosity an asset? 
 Being curious means that you are eager to learn and discover new things. Being a lifelong learner has benefits that extend outside of the classroom. We continue to seek out more knowledge in our personal lives, work roles, and in higher education. Can you think of a situation where being curious is helpful? How can we use curiosity to better ourselves? 

 How do we discover our options? Why is identifying our options important? 
 We can use our innate curiosity to discover the options that we have in life. Without figuring out our options, we won’t know the full extent of our opportunities in life. If we are never presented with what can be, we will always stay where we are. Have you considered the courses you will take next year? Have you considered your options after high school?
