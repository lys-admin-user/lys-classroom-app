The System-Level Interdependency Engine
No department or role operates in isolation. The company maintains operational stability
through three strict interdependency loops:
 1.​ The Product-Marketing Loop: The Sales Coordinator and Growth Sales Managers log
     customer feature gaps and user objections directly in HubSpot. The Ed Tech Product
     Manager synthesizes these logs weekly into technical spec updates for Web Developer
     One and the CTO.
 2.​ The Content-Implementation Loop: Implementation Specialists (RICs) observe live
     classroom usage of "Bricks" and report engagement metrics to the Director of Educator
     Success. This role directs Instructional Designers and Author Teachers to refine
     curriculum modules based on student-level data.
 3.​ The Revenue-Logistics Loop: The Grants and Institutional Funding Manager secures
     public and private funding, which the Global Director of Adoption & Revenue (GDAR)
     and Regional Directors (RDIPs) leverage to discount school district onboarding and
     prepare the infrastructure for the distant-future N.E.S.T. Site Operations.
